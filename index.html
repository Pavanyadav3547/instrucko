CURRICULUM FOR AGES 3 +

  • Sound in CVC words
  • Digital operations- click, drag
  • Visualising scenes
  • Action words (-ing words)
  • Read words containing taught GPCs and –s, –es, –ing, –ed, –er and –est endings
  • Position of objects
  • Sound out and blend graphemes
  • Read phonically decodable two-syllable and three-syllable words

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 4 +

  • Segment syllables
  • Animation graphics
  • Rhyming words
  • Identify scene elements
  • Naming words- (person and place)
  • Prefix, suffix
  • Capitalisation
  • Write simple sentences

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 5 +

  • Blend onset and rime
  • Alphabetical order
  • Opposite words
  • Questions and statements identification
  • Nouns, pronouns, adverbs
  • Using the present and past tenses, as well as the progressive form
  • Combining words to make sentences
  • Putting sentences together to make small narratives

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 6 +

  • Segment and blend CVC words
  • Segment and blend VCC words
  • Compare and contrast digital text
  • Knowing and using statements, questions, exclamations, and commands
  • Using apostrophes and commas to mark where letters are missing and to mark possession
  • Joining clauses using a range of conjunctions
  • Exclamations and questions

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 7 +

  • Segment and blend CVCC words
  • Differentiating genres- folktales, articles, fables
  • Compare and contrast digital photos
  • Expressing time, place, and cause with conjunctions, adverbs, and prepositions
  • Using the possessive apostrophe with regular and irregular plurals
  • Spelling more homophones
  • Knowing the plural and possessive -s

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 8 +

  • Common consonant digraphs
  • Using fronted adverbials
  • Using paragraphs to organise ideas
  • Using inverted commas to punctuate speech
  • Digital text communication
  • Word families
  • Using brackets, dashes, or commas to indicate parenthesis

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 9 +

  • Variant vowel digraphs
  • Linking ideas across paragraphs using adverbials of time, place, and number, or tense choices
  • Showing degrees of possibility using adverbs or modal verbs
  • Using words and phrases to build cohesion within a paragraph
  • Using morphology and etymology in spelling
  • Using brackets, dashes, or commas to indicate parenthesis
  • Verb tenses and conjunctions
  • Idioms and Synonyms

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 10 +

  • Sound in CVC words
  • Digital operations- click, drag
  • Visualising scenes
  • Action words (-ing words)
  • Read words containing taught GPCs and –s, –es, –ing, –ed, –er and –est endings
  • Read phonically decodable two-syllable and three-syllable words

CURRICULUM FOR AGES 11 +

  • Sound in CVC words
  • Digital operations- click, drag
  • Visualising scenes
  • Action words (-ing words)
  • Read words containing taught GPCs and –s, –es, –ing, –ed, –er and –est endings
  • Read phonically decodable two-syllable and three-syllable words
